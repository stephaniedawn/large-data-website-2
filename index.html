Popular Fall Trends 2013

Latest Blog Post:

October 7th, 2013

This is always a popular topic— Seasonal Hair Trends, so here it is!

What's trending this Fall:

  • Middle part with long, slightly-layered curled hair.
  • Whispy side-bangs matched with a long bob.
  • Ombre is still trending but in deeper, auburn-y brown tones or golden rich blondes.
  • Chestnut browns, golden deep blondes, and subtle peek-a-boo tones on deeper brunettes.
  • The top-knot or large bun is still a go this season because of it's simplicity and femininity.

YouTube is a great place to look for how-tos or to gather inspiration!
