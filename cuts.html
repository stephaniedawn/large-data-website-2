Angled Bob:

September 25th, 2013

Ahhh, the angled bob.

This cut will forever be popular. Victoria Beckham surely made it the popular first with her sleek platinum locks.

This haircut is great on most face shapes. You can always change the angle or length to suit the person better.
The beauty of this cut is how the back is cropped so short, you don't even have to style it.
Unless you're one of the unfortunate few who have crazy bed head in the mornings. Either way, nothing a little water can't fix.
It's just a stylish, feminine cut that's great for on-the-go women.
Countless times I've give some variation of the angled bob to a new mother, or mom-to-be.

Half-shaved Head:

August 20th, 2013

This has always been a curious look to me.

I remember years ago being in highschool and seeing a girl walk past me. I did a double take. The first time I had seen the half-head shave with long hair.

I'll admit, on her, it was stunning.
It's a daring look, very edgy, especially when you have long hair.
If you have the guts, I say go for it.
The only issue is: the grow out faze. Be aware that if you grow tired of the looking, growing it out will look ridiculous for awhile.
I also suggest you have medium-to-thick hair if you're looking into doing it. (It will help the grow-out stage, and overally look better. If you're hair is too fine, it could look like you're just losing your hair. To be brutally honest.
Famous male example: Sonny Moore of Skrillex.
